ABOUT

U WANT TO KNOW MORE ABOUT US?

lolli top is a Aarhus, Denmark, based brand founded by Nanna in 2020. the ambition is to create a hybrid universe of styles and art. Every style are unique and when you buy a lolli top you'll be the only one having that specific top.

All tops are produced according to a sustainable thinking, most of the materials are recycled or upcycled

Why not do what you can do for the enviroment? the vision is to be 100% sustainable and to be part of more creative projects in the future.
