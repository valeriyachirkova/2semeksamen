PRODUCTION

TAKE A LOOK AND WATCH OUR AMAZING PRODUCTION PROCESS!

At first, we make some LOL sketchings by hand and designing some ideas. Y2K aesthetic style is Lolli Tops favorite inspiration source.

We are conscious about buying fabric. Mostly we find the fabric in second hand stores.

Then the top is being cut and sewed. Some of the tops are upcycled or recycled, some of them are made from scratch.

The final step is - decorating the tops. Sewing on the diamonds, rhinestones, chains etc. Now you have your one of one unique and sustainable top!
